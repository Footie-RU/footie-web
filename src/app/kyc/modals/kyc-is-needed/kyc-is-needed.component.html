Why KYC is Important

To ensure a secure and trustworthy platform, we require all users to complete the KYC (Know Your Customer) process. KYC helps us verify the identity of users and prevents fraudulent activity, ensuring that only verified individuals can use our services.

By submitting your KYC documents, you contribute to maintaining a safe environment for all users. This verification also helps us comply with regulatory requirements and industry standards.

Please upload your selfie, international passport, and Russian passport to complete the verification. Once your documents are reviewed, you'll be notified of your approval status.
